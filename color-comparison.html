Summary — All 5 color directions at a glance For Dr. Z's final decision

Color Direction Summary

1. Coral

#F15B5C

2. Cyan/Teal

#00A2B0

3. Yellow

#FFB533

4. Black

#000000

5. Deep Navy ★

#025870

1. Coral — Bold & Urgent

High energy — great for ads & conversion

100% on-brand color

Red-adjacent — can feel alarming in health context

CTA button disappears into background

2. Cyan/Teal — Fresh & Medical

Clean medical feel — associated with health

Coral CTA pops well against it

Can feel sterile or clinical — less warm

Close to existing teal in app — less differentiation

3. Yellow — Warm & Energetic

Warm and approachable — friendly feel

Unique — not many health apps use yellow

Hardest to read on — contrast issues

Least premium feeling of all options

4. Black — Bold & Premium

Very premium — bold statement

Brand colors pop brilliantly on black

Can feel cold or intimidating in health context

Not in brand kit — needs Dr. Z approval

5. Deep Navy ★ Recommended

Premium, trustworthy, authoritative

Coral CTA has maximum contrast & impact

Matches "calm authority" brand voice perfectly

Not in official kit — needs Dr. Z approval

Strategic recommendation for Dr. Z

All five options use the same brand colors — the only difference is which color anchors the background. Our strong recommendation is Option 5 (Deep Navy) for the app's primary experience. It's the only option where every other brand color — coral, teal, and gold — can be used strategically without competing. The coral CTA button pops harder against navy than against any other background.

If Dr. Z wants to stay strictly within the official brand kit without adding navy, Option 2 (Cyan/Teal) is the next strongest choice — it's clean, medical, and lets coral do the conversion work.

⚑ Options 4 (Black) and 5 (Deep Navy) are not in the official brand kit and require Dr. Z's formal approval before the dev team adopts them. Options 1, 2, and 3 are all official brand colors.
