Your question

Why am I so tired even if the day is cold?

Mar 11, 20263 explanations returned

Your 3 possible explanations

Possible patterns — not conclusions.

1

Sleep and circadian factors

Cold temperatures can disrupt your body's natural sleep-wake cycle. When ambient temperature drops, your body works harder to regulate core temperature overnight, which can fragment sleep quality even if total sleep hours seem adequate.

What to ask your provider:

"Could my sleep architecture be affected by temperature changes? Should we check melatonin or cortisol levels?"

2

Low activity and nutrition

Reduced daylight in colder months lowers serotonin and vitamin D production, both of which directly affect energy levels.

What to ask your provider:

"Can we check my vitamin D, B12, and iron levels?"

3

Medical causes to consider

Thyroid dysfunction and seasonal affective patterns are two commonly overlooked contributors to cold-weather fatigue.

What to ask your provider:

"Could we run a full thyroid panel including T3, T4, and TSH?"

Help & FAQ

Everything you need to know about DiagnoseThis.

What is DiagnoseThis?

+

DiagnoseThis helps you understand what your body may be trying to say — before you're dismissed, overwhelmed, or told to stop asking questions. It organizes what you're experiencing, recognizes patterns, and helps you ask better, more grounded questions with real providers.

How does the Ask WHY feature work?

+

Type your health question or symptom in plain language. DiagnoseThis analyzes it through a functional medicine lens and returns 3 possible explanations — each with a plain language summary and suggested questions to bring to your provider. Free accounts get 3 WHYs per month.

Is this a replacement for medical advice?

+

No. DiagnoseThis does not diagnose disease, prescribe treatment, or replace your clinician. It helps you understand what's happening well enough to be taken seriously — so you can show up informed, not unsure.

What's the difference between See WHY and The WHY?

+

See WHY is a medium depth report — pattern analysis, suggested labs, downloadable PDF. The WHY is the full clinical reasoning report — everything in See WHY plus actionable next steps and a provider script.

Is my health information private?

+

Yes. Your health questions and personal information are encrypted and never shared with third parties.

How do I cancel my subscription?

+

You can cancel anytime from your Profile & Settings page. Click "Manage Subscription" and follow the steps. You'll retain full access until the end of your current billing period — no penalties, no runaround.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help — real people, real answers.
