About DiagnoseThis — how it works, what it costs, and what it's not.

Categories

DiagnoseThis helps you understand what your body may be trying to say — before you're dismissed, overwhelmed, or told to stop asking questions. It organizes what you're experiencing, recognizes patterns, and helps you ask better, more grounded questions with real providers.

Type your health question or symptom in plain language. DiagnoseThis analyzes it through a functional medicine lens and returns 3 possible explanations — each with a plain language summary and suggested questions to bring to your provider. Free accounts get 3 WHYs per month.

No. DiagnoseThis does not diagnose disease, prescribe treatment, or replace your clinician. It helps you understand what's happening well enough to be taken seriously — so you can show up informed, not unsure.

See WHY is a medium depth report — pattern analysis, suggested labs, downloadable PDF. The WHY is the full clinical reasoning report — everything in See WHY plus actionable next steps and a provider script that your doctor actually can't ignore.

Yes. Your health questions and personal information are encrypted and never shared with third parties. Your health data is yours — full stop.

You can cancel anytime from your Profile & Settings page. Click "Manage Subscription" and follow the steps. You'll retain full access until the end of your current billing period — no penalties, no runaround.

Free accounts get 3 WHYs per month. Your first WHY is always free — no credit card required. Upgrade to See WHY or The WHY for unlimited WHYs plus deeper reports.

DiagnoseThis is built and reviewed by Dr. Z and a team of licensed functional medicine clinicians. Every explanation is grounded in real clinical experience — not just algorithms.
