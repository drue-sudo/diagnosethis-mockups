About DiagnoseThis

This Exists Because
Something Was Missing

Not more information. Not more opinions. Not more noise.
Clarity.

The gap no one talks about

Most people don't start in a doctor's office. They start with a search.

Something feels off. They look for answers. And then they hear: "Everything looks normal." But their life doesn't feel normal. That gap — between "nothing dangerous showed up" and "I still don't feel okay" — is where people begin to doubt themselves.

You're not broken

Most systems are built for speed. Symptoms get separated. Stories get shortened.

And anything that doesn't fit clean criteria gets labeled "fine." But "fine" isn't an explanation. And it's not a plan.

You don't need more guessing. You need structure.

What this actually is

DiagnoseThis doesn't diagnose conditions. It does something different.

Organize

Organize what you're experiencing into something structured and readable.

Recognize

Recognize patterns across your symptoms that a quick appointment might miss.

Ask better

Prepare for real conversations with real providers — questions they can't ignore.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters.

We believe in transparency and security. Here's how we protect your data and respect your privacy rights.

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

At DiagnoseThis, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and encrypted password to secure your access.

Question history

Your WHY questions and the AI's responses are stored securely. We respect your sensitive queries and minimize data retention on our servers.

Usage data

We may collect anonymous analytics to help us improve the app's performance and user experience.

How we use your information

Provide and maintain service
Authenticate your identity
Improve user experience
Send important updates

Data security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We continuously monitor for vulnerabilities.

Third-party sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your health data is yours, and we intend to keep it that way.

Have questions about our privacy practices?

Our privacy team is here to help.
