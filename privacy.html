Overview

At DiagnoseThis, we take your privacy seriously. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and encrypted password to secure your access.

Question history

Your WHY questions and the AI's responses are stored securely. We respect your sensitive queries and minimize data retention on our servers.

Usage data

We may collect anonymous analytics to help us improve the app's performance and user experience.

How we use your information

Provide and maintain service

Authenticate your identity

Improve user experience

Send important updates

Data security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We continuously monitor for vulnerabilities and update our systems to maintain the highest standards of protection.

Third-party sharing

Our commitment

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your health data is yours, and we intend to keep it that way.

Have questions about our privacy practices?

Our privacy team is here to help.
